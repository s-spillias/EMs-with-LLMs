Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                     

I propose incorporating a density-dependent effect on COTS predation. The current Holling type II functional response assumes that predation rate increases linearly with COTS density up to a saturation 
point. However, at very high COTS densities, interference among predators (e.g., competition for prey, reduced foraging efficiency) can lead to a decline in per capita predation rate. This can be       
implemented by modifying the Holling type II functional response to include a term that reduces predation efficiency at high COTS densities. This could help to better capture the rapid population       
explosions and subsequent declines observed in COTS outbreaks.                                                                                                                                            

Mathematical Implementation:                                                                                                                                                                              

Modify the Holling type II functional response to include a density-dependent term:                                                                                                                       

                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 predation_rate = p_cots * cots_density / (K1_cots + cots_density + (cots_density^2 / K2_cots))
