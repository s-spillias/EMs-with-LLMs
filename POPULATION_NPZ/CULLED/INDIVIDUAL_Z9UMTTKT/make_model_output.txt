 We enhanced the ecological model by incorporating light   
 limitation effects on phytoplankton growth. This addition

 \subsection{Light Limitation Implementation}              
 We introduced a simplified seasonal light cycle using a   
 sinusoidal function with an annual period. The relative   
 light intensity (I\_rel) varies between 0 and 1 throughou 
 the year. The uptake rate is then modified by a

     \item More realistic nutrient-phytoplankton coupling

 oscillations visible in the historical data.
