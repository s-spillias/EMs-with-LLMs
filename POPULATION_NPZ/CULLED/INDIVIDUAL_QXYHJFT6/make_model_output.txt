 \section{Ecological Mechanism:          
 Nutrient-Dependent Growth Efficiency}   
                                         
 We introduced a competitive adaptation  
 mechanism to better represent how       
 phytoplankton respond to nutrient       
 limitation. The new term $\gamma_P$     
 modifies nutrient uptake efficiency bas 
 on nutrient availability:               
                                         
 \[                                      
 \text{comp\_factor} = 1 + \gamma_P      
 \frac{K_N}{N + K_N}                     
 \]                                      
                                         
 This represents:                        
 \begin{itemize}                         
 \item Enhanced uptake efficiency when   
 nutrients are scarce ($N \ll K_N$)      
 \item Return to baseline efficiency whe 
 nutrients are abundant ($N \gg K_N$)    
 \end{itemize}                           
                                         
 This mechanism is ecologically justifie 
 by:                                     
 \begin{itemize}                         
 \item Observed physiological adaptation 
 in nutrient-limited conditions          
 \item Competitive pressure selecting fo 
 efficient resource use                  
 \item Field evidence of enhanced uptake 
 during nutrient stress                  
 \end{itemize}                           
                                         
 The modification should improve model f 
 by:                                     
 \begin{itemize}                         
 \item Better capturing bloom dynamics   
 when nutrients become available         
 \item More realistic representation of  
 phytoplankton adaptation                
 \item Improved nutrient-biomass couplin 
 in the model                            
 \end{itemize}
