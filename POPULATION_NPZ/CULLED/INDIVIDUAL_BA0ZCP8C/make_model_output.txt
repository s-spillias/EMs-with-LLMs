 This model enhancement introduces nutrient-dependent phytoplankton growth

 The efficiency term eta_N increases as nutrient concentrations decrease,

 - Baseline efficiency (eta_N = 1) under nutrient-replete conditions

 - Smooth transition using the same half-saturation constant K_N as nutrient    
 uptake

 phytoplankton populations under nutrient stress, potentially improving the fit 
 to observed dynamics.
