 metabolic processes in marine ecosystems.

 \[ f(T) = \exp(\frac{E_a}{k}(\frac{1}{T_{ref}} -

 \begin{itemize}                                           
 \item $E_a$ is the activation energy (eV)

 \item $T$ is the ambient temperature (K)                  
 \end{itemize}
