 1. The existing model captures outbreak events via a logistic trigger based on lagged COTS abundance.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
 2. However, the linear predation term (using gamma * cots_pred * (fast+slow)) oversimplifies real ecological dynamics.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
 3. Empirical evidence suggests that at high COTS densities, predation efficiency saturates due to interference or limited handling time.

This modification should improve the model’s ability to capture the boom-bust cycles and outbreak dynamics noted in the project context.
