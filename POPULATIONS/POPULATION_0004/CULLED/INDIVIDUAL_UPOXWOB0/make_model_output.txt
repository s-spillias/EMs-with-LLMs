 1 Assessment & Rationale:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
   The current model captures boom‐bust behavior using logistic growth modulated by an outbreak indicator. However, it overlooks the ecological feedback that as coral cover (both fast‐ and slow‐growing) declines, the available resources for COTS are reduced, which could dampen further growth. In reality, reduced coral prey should limit COTS growth during or following an   
   outbreak. To address this, we introduce a new parameter resource_availability_coef and incorporate a multiplicative factor in the logistic growth term that scales with the combined previous coral cover (normalized to a maximum of 200% cover).

 Assessment:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
 The current model effectively reproduces boom‐bust cycles using logistic growth combined with a smooth outbreak indicator. However, it oversimplifies resource availability by not considering that reduced coral cover limits the food available for Crown‐of‐Thorns starfish. Ecologically, as coral cover (both fast and slow) decreases, resource limitation should dampen COTS   
 growth.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
 To capture this feedback, a new resource availability term is introduced in the COTS logistic growth equation. This term calculates the normalized combined coral cover (fast_prev + slow_prev divided by 200) and scales it by a new parameter `resource_availability_coef`. This modification enhances model realism by linking coral depletion to reduced COTS growth potential.
