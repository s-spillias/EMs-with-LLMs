1. Model Fit and Project Context                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       

 • The model uses lognormal likelihoods for COTS, fast, and slow coral, and includes outbreak triggers, resource limitation, and selective predation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
 • The process model is mechanistically rich: COTS growth is limited by coral, outbreaks are triggered by environmental cues, and coral groups compete for space and are selectively predated.                                                                                                                                                                                         
 • However, the model currently uses a linear resource limitation for coral on COTS growth (i.e., coral_avail / (coral_avail + kappa_coral)), which may not fully capture the threshold or nonlinear effects observed in real boom-bust COTS outbreaks.                                                                                                                                

2. Missing or Oversimplified Processes                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 

 • The main ecological process that may be oversimplified is the effect of coral availability on COTS population growth. In reality, COTS outbreaks often show a sharp, threshold-like response to coral cover: below a certain coral threshold, COTS populations cannot sustain outbreaks, but above it, rapid population growth is possible.                                         
 • The current saturating function is monotonic and smooth, but may not be steep enough to capture this threshold effect.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              

3. Proposed Ecological Improvement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     

 • Introduce a Hill-type (sigmoidal) function for coral limitation on COTS growth: This allows for a flexible, nonlinear response, where the Hill coefficient controls the steepness of the transition from low to high COTS growth as coral availability increases.                                                                                                                   
 • This is ecologically justified: empirical studies show that COTS require a minimum coral cover to sustain outbreaks, and the response is often highly nonlinear.                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Mathematically, this is similar to the Hill function already used for predation efficiency, but applied to the coral limitation term.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               

Implementation Plan                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    

 • Add a new parameter: hill_n_coral (Hill coefficient for coral limitation on COTS growth).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
 • Update the coral limitation term in the COTS growth equation to use a Hill function:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
    • Replace: coral_limitation = coral_avail / (coral_avail + kappa_coral);
