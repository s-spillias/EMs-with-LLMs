Proposed Ecological Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Approach:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
Introduce a nonlinear (saturating) resource limitation for COTS population growth, reflecting that COTS population growth should be strongly limited when coral cover is low, but less so when coral is abundant. This is ecologically justified: COTS larvae and juveniles require sufficient coral for survival and recruitment, and outbreaks cannot be sustained if coral is       
depleted.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              

Mathematical Form:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
Modify the COTS logistic growth term to include a saturating function of coral availability, e.g.:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
 growth = r_cots * cots_pred(t-1) * (1 - cots_pred(t-1)/K_cots) * (coral_avail / (coral_avail + kappa_coral))                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       

where kappa_coral is a new parameter controlling the half-saturation of coral limitation on COTS growth.
