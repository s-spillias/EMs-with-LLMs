This does not adequately capture the resource limitation in predation of coral by COTS.                                                                                         
 Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 Introduce a saturating (Holling type II) functional response in the predation term. Specifically, modify the term so that the growth contribution from coral availability follows:                                                                                                                                                                                                    
   (attack_rate * coral) / (K + coral)
