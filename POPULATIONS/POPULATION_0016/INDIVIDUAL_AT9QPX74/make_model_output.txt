Chosen Improvement: Add an Allee Effect to COTS Recruitment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            

Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
An Allee effect (reduced recruitment at low COTS densities) is ecologically justified: COTS require a minimum density for successful fertilization, and mate limitation is well-documented. Including an Allee effect can help the model better capture the abrupt onset and collapse of outbreaks, as well as periods of low abundance, thus improving the timing and magnitude of    
boom-bust cycles.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      

Mathematical Form:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
Modify the COTS recruitment term to include an Allee effect, e.g.:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
 COTS recruitment ∝ cots_prev * (cots_prev / (A + cots_prev))                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       

where A is the Allee threshold parameter (new, positive). This term reduces recruitment at low COTS densities, but has little effect at high densities.
