 • However, the duration and magnitude of outbreaks may be sensitive to how predation rates respond to coral availability and COTS density.

Missing or Oversimplified Processes

4. Chosen Improvement: Density-Dependent Predation Efficiency                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          

 • I propose to add a density-dependent efficiency term to the COTS predation rates (alpha_fast, alpha_slow), so that per capita predation declines at high COTS densities.

 • Where gamma_cots is a new parameter (>=0) controlling the strength of interference.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • This modification is simple, ecologically justified, and can improve the model's ability to capture the decline phase and outbreak duration.
