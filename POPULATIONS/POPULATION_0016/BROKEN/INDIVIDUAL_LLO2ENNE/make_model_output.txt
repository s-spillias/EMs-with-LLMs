 2 Addressing Project Context:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
   The model captures the boom-bust dynamics of COTS via density-dependent recruitment (logistic term), temperature effects, and immigration. It includes selective predation on fast and slow corals, and resource limitation for both COTS and corals. However, the only feedback from coral to COTS is via carrying capacity and predation rates; there is no explicit feedback from
   coral cover to COTS recruitment or survival, other than through predation terms.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
 3 Missing or Oversimplified Processes:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
    • Resource Limitation: The COTS recruitment is limited by COTS density (logistic), but not directly by coral availability. In reality, COTS larvae and juveniles depend on coral cover for settlement and survival, especially Acropora.                                                                                                                                           
    • Feedback Mechanisms: There is no direct feedback from coral depletion to COTS recruitment/survival, which is ecologically important for sustaining or collapsing outbreaks.                                                                                                                                                                                                      
    • Nonlinearities: The model uses Michaelis-Menten predation, but COTS recruitment is only limited by COTS density, not by food (coral) availability.                                                                                                                                                                                                                               

Proposed Ecological Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
Add a saturating (Michaelis-Menten) dependence of COTS recruitment on total coral cover (fast + slow).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 

 • Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
   Outbreaks are sustained only when sufficient coral prey is available. When coral cover is low, COTS recruitment and survival should be reduced, providing a negative feedback that helps end outbreaks and prevents unrealistic persistence at high COTS densities when coral is depleted.                                                                                          
 • Mathematical Form:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
   Modify the COTS recruitment term to include a saturating function of total coral cover:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             
   recruit_cots = r_cots * cots_pred(t-1) * (1 - cots_pred(t-1)/K_cots) * temp_eff_cots * (coral_total/(h_coral + coral_total))                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
   where coral_total = fast_pred(t-1) + slow_pred(t-1) and h_coral is a new half-saturation parameter.
