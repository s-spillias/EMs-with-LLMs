Assessment of the Current Model                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        

 1 Model Fit to Data:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
   The model uses lognormal likelihoods for COTS, fast coral, and slow coral, and includes observation error. Without the actual residuals file, I cannot quantitatively assess fit, but the structure is appropriate for positive-valued ecological data. The model includes reporting of predicted time series for comparison with observations.                                     
 2 Addressing Project Context:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
   The model captures the main features of COTS outbreaks:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             

 • Logistic growth for COTS, modulated by coral cover (outbreak threshold) and SST.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Immigration of COTS larvae.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
 • Selective predation on fast and slow-growing corals, with different rates and efficiencies.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
 • Coral recovery and resource limitation via carrying capacities.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
 • Selectivity for coral types via logit-transformed parameters.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       

This structure allows for boom-bust cycles and selective predation, as required by the project context.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                

 3 Potential Missing or Oversimplified Processes:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      

 • Resource Limitation: The model uses a logistic term for COTS and for coral, but the COTS logistic term is only limited by its own abundance and a coral outbreak threshold.                                                                                                                                                                                                         
 • Feedbacks: There is no explicit negative feedback from coral depletion on COTS growth, except via the outbreak threshold (a smooth step function).                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
 • Predation Saturation: The Holling Type II functional response is used, which is appropriate.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 • Environmental Modifiers: SST is included as a multiplicative effect on COTS growth.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • Variable Efficiency: Assimilation efficiencies are constant.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 • Competition: There is no explicit competition between COTS and other benthic organisms or among coral types, except via summed cover in the logistic terms.                                                                                                                                                                                                                         

Key Ecological Process for Improvement                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 

The outbreak threshold is currently implemented as a smooth step function of total coral cover. However, this may be too simplistic:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   

 • It does not allow for a gradual, saturating effect of coral availability on COTS population growth.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • In reality, COTS population growth should be limited by coral food availability in a continuous, saturating manner, not just a threshold.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           

Proposed Improvement: Resource Limitation via Saturating Food Function                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 

Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         

 • Ecologically, COTS outbreaks are sustained only when sufficient coral prey is available.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
 • A saturating function (e.g., Michaelis-Menten or Monod) of total coral cover provides a more realistic, gradual limitation on COTS growth, rather than a sharp threshold.                                                                                                                                                                                                           
 • This allows the model to better capture the decline phase of outbreaks as coral is depleted, and prevents unrealistically high COTS growth when coral is scarce but above the threshold.                                                                                                                                                                                            

Implementation Plan:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   

 • Replace the outbreak threshold effect with a saturating function of total coral cover:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
   food_lim = coral_total_prev / (coral_total_prev + K_food)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
   where K_food is a new parameter representing the half-saturation constant for food limitation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
 • Remove or replace the current outbreak threshold effect in the COTS growth equation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
 • Add log_K_food to parameters.json with justification.
