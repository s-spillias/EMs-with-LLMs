 Assessment:                                                                                                                                                                                                           
 The current model captures the boom-bust dynamics of COTS through logistic growth and a smooth outbreak-triggered decline. However, it does not account for the depletion of coral resourcesâ€”a key ecological process 
 driving outbreak busts. Without linking food availability to COTS growth, the model may overpredict outbreak persistence.                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
 Chosen Improvement:

 this feedback. This ensures that when coral cover declines, reduced food availability naturally limits COTS growth, leading to more ecologically consistent boom-bust cycles.
