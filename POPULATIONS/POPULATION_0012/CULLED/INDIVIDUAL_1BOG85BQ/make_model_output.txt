Assessment & Chosen Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
After reviewing the model and its residuals (observed versus predicted values), it appears that the outbreak‐trigger mechanism for COTS is too linear. Although the current logistic function with a threshold captures some aspects of outbreak initiation, it does not fully reflect the cooperative, non‐linear effects observed ecologically. In many systems, small increases in  
density near a critical threshold can lead to disproportionate outbreak initiation. To capture this, we introduce a quadratic term into the outbreak trigger calculation via a new parameter, outbreak_poly_modifier. This parameter allows the model to incorporate a non‐linear (polynomial) cooperative effect on outbreak initiation, thereby enhancing the fit and better         
addressing the complex eco-dynamics behind rapid COTS outbreaks.
