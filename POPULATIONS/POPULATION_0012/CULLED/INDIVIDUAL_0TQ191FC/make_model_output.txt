 This update was motivated by an assessment of the model, which indicated that while resource limitation via coral availability was    
 incorporated into COTS growth, the outbreak trigger relied solely on COTS density relative to carrying capacity. Ecologically, health 
 coral cover can help stabilize COTS populations by indirectly raising the threshold for outbreak initiation. To capture this dynamic  
 feedback, I introduced the "coral_outbreak_modifier" parameter. This parameter adjusts the effective outbreak threshold based on the  
 total coral cover (the sum of fast- and slow-growing coral predictions), thereby providing a more nuanced representation of the       
 boom-bust cycles observed in COTS outbreaks.
