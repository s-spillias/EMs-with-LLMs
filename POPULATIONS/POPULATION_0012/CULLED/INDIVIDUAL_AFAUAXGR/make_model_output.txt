 decrease nonlinearly, leading to a sharper reduction in growth.

 applying an exponent to the resource factor, the model can represent a more abrupt decline in COTS population growth when coral cover 
 is severely reduced, which better reflects the ecological dynamics observed during outbreak events.                                   
                                                                                                                                       
 This modification improves the ecological realism of the model without introducing data leakage (only using past state values in      
 predictions).
