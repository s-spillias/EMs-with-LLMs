Assessment & Chosen Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                       
The current model captures the timing, magnitude, and duration of COTS outbreaks well using logistic growth and a smooth outbreak-trigger function. However, while the model selectively penalizes fast and slow coral 
via predation, it uses a fixed coral predation efficiency. In reality, environmental conditions (e.g. temperature anomalies, water quality) often modulate predator efficiency. To better represent the ecological     
dynamics described in the project context we add a new parameter, pred_env_modifier, that nonlinearly modulates coral predation losses. This change allows the model to capture variability in predation intensity     
under different environmental conditions, thereby improving the fit to outbreak residuals without introducing data leakage.
