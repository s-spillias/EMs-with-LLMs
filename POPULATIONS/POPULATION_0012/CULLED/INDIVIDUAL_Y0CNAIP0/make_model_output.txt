 limitation on growth is incorporated via a Michaelisâ€“Menten function, the decline due to outbreaks increases indefinitely with COTS   
 density. Ecologically, it is expected that very high densities lead to saturation effects (e.g., due to resource depletion or predato 
 satiation) that limit the decline rate.                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                       
 Improvement:                                                                                                                          
 Introduce a new parameter, "outbreak_decline_saturation", to incorporate a density-dependent saturation in the outbreak decline term. 
 This modification ensures that the strength of population collapse does not unrealistically increase at very high densities, better   
 capturing the boom-bust nature of COTS outbreaks in accordance with ecological processes.
