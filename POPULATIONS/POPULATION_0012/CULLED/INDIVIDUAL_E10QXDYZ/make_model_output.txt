Assessment & Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
The current model captures the boom‐bust dynamics of COTS outbreaks but does not include an indirect feedback from the coral community. In nature, a relatively healthy coral cover may delay or inhibit outbreak onset by providing ecosystem resilience. To better capture this, we add a new parameter coral_outbreak_inhibition that modulates the outbreak trigger. The outbreak  
trigger function is modified so that high combined coral cover (fast and slow) reduces the effective trigger, thereby delaying outbreaks under healthy reef conditions. This added dynamic strengthens the biological realism without sacrificing the model’s structure and avoids any data leakage by always using previous time step values.
