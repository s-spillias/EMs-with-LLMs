 • The current model does a reasonable job reproducing the basic boom‐bust outbreak dynamics of COTS and the associated coral impacts.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • However, residual analysis suggests that at high COTS densities the model over‐predicts predation losses on coral. This may be due to the fixed predation efficiency not capturing intraspecific interference among COTS. In nature, very high densities could lead to interference (e.g. competition, disruption) that results in lower effective predation rates.                 

Chosen Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
We introduce a new variable predation efficiency term via an interference coefficient. This parameter (named interference_coefficient) will modulate the predation terms in both fast‐ and slow‐growing coral equations using the expression:                                                                                                                                          
  interference_factor = 1 / (1 + interference_coefficient * COTS[t-1])                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
This change will ensure that as COTS densities (from the previous timestep) rise, the effective predation rate decreases—better representing the ecological feedback and interference at high densities.
