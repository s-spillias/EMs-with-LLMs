Assessment and chosen improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     

 1 Our assessment revealed that although the current model reproduces the boom‐bust dynamics of COTS outbreaks and their impact on corals, the coral recovery process is modeled as a linear function. In reality, coral recovery is often non‐linear – when coral cover is very low, recovery can be disproportionately slow due to limitations in recruitment and feedback           
   mechanisms.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
 2 To better capture this ecological process, we are introducing a density‐dependent, non‐linear recovery mechanism. We do this by adding a new parameter named coral_recovery_nonlinearity that modulates the recovery term; when its value is greater than 1, coral recovery slows down more at lower cover values, thereby enhancing the boom–bust dynamics and better reflecting   
   ecological resilience.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
 3 The recovery terms for fast-growing and slow-growing corals in the model will be multiplied by a power function, pow((100 - current_cover) / 100, coral_recovery_nonlinearity). This ensures that when coral cover is low, recovery is reduced, matching the ecological context.
