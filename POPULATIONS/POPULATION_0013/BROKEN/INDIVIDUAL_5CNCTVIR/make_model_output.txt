Chosen Improvement: Resource-Dependent COTS Mortality                                                                                                                                                                  

Ecological Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                              
To better capture the rapid decline of COTS populations after outbreaks (the "bust" phase), I will implement a resource-dependent mortality term for COTS. When total coral cover (especially fast coral) is low, COTS 
mortality increases due to starvation and lack of food. This negative feedback is well-supported in the literature and is critical for reproducing realistic outbreak cycles.                                          

Mathematical Implementation:                                                                                                                                                                                           

 • Add a new parameter: gamma_m_cots (strength of resource-dependent mortality).                                                                                                                                       
 • COTS mortality rate becomes:                                                                                                                                                                                        
   m_cots_eff = m_cots + gamma_m_cots * (1 - coral_total_prev / K_coral)                                                                                                                                               
   (i.e., mortality increases as coral cover declines).
