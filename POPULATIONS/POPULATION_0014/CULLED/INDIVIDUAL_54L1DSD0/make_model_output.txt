 the existing formulation may oversimplify the feedback mechanism between coral availability and COTS growth.

 growth boost (outbreak_boost) is applied to the COTS population. This adjustment simulates reduced predation pressure and resource limitation effects, reflecting the ecological reality that severely depleted coral 
 cover can lead to rapid COTS population explosions.                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
 This improvement, while adding slight complexity, is justified by observed outbreak behavior and enhances the model's ability to predict outbreak timing, magnitude, and duration.
