                                                                                              Proposed Change: Photoinhibition                                                                                              

I will implement the Steele (1962) formulation for light-limited growth, which includes both light limitation at low intensities and photoinhibition at high intensities:                                                   

Light limitation factor = (I/I_opt) × exp(1 - I/I_opt)                                                                                                                                                                      

Where:                                                                                                                                                                                                                      

 • I = light intensity                                                                                                                                                                                                      
 • I_opt = optimal light intensity for maximum photosynthesis                                                                                                                                                               

This creates a peaked response: growth increases with light up to I_opt, then decreases at higher intensities due to photoinhibition.
