Current Structure:                                                                                                                                                                                                          

 • NPZ (Nutrient-Phytoplankton-Zooplankton) model with Monod kinetics for nutrient uptake and Holling Type II functional response for grazing                                                                               
 • Linear mortality terms for both P and Z                                                                                                                                                                                  
 • Simple nutrient recycling from mortality                                                                                                                                                                                 

Key Issues Identified:                                                                                                                                                                                                      

 1 Missing Light Limitation: The model only considers nutrient limitation for phytoplankton growth, but in oceanic mixed layers, light is often co-limiting with nutrients. This is a critical oversight for realistic      
   plankton dynamics.                                                                                                                                                                                                       
 2 Oversimplified Mortality: Linear mortality doesn't capture density-dependent effects or predation pressure on zooplankton from higher trophic levels.                                                                    
 3 Parameter Values: The efficiency parameters (epsilon_P, epsilon_Z, gamma) are initialized at 0.0 (logit scale), which transforms to 0.5 on the bounded scale. This is reasonable but these should be estimated from data.

Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

I will add light limitation to phytoplankton growth, which is essential for oceanic mixed layer dynamics. Phytoplankton growth should be limited by both nutrients AND light availability. I'll implement this using a      
multiplicative Monod-type formulation where growth is limited by the minimum of nutrient and light availability.                                                                                                            

This addresses a fundamental ecological process: in the mixed layer, light intensity varies with depth and season, and phytoplankton growth is typically co-limited by both light and nutrients. This is particularly       
important for capturing spring blooms and seasonal dynamics.
