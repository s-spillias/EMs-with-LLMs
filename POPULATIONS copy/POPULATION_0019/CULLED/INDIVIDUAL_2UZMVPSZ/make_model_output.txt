 +1. Light availability varies with mixed layer depth, season, and latitude                                                                                                                                                 
 +2. Deep mixing events can light-limit phytoplankton even when nutrients are abundant                                                                                                                                      
 +3. Vertical position in the water column affects light exposure                                                                                                                                                           
 +4. This is a fundamental control on primary production in marine systems                                                                                                                                                  
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +ECOLOGICAL IMPROVEMENT:                                                                                                                                                                                                   
 +Add light limitation to phytoplankton growth using a multiplicative dual-limitation framework:                                                                                                                            
 +- Implement Monod kinetics for light limitation: I / (K_I + I)                                                                                                                                                            
 +- Light intensity (I) will be a DATA_VECTOR input representing average mixed layer light

 +- Growth becomes: r_P * [N/(K_N + N)] * [I/(K_I + I)] * P                                                                                                                                                                 
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +This creates a realistic representation where phytoplankton growth is limited by whichever resource (nutrient or light) is most scarce, following Liebig's Law of the Minimum in its multiplicative approximation.        
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +MATHEMATICAL JUSTIFICATION:                                                                                                                                                                                               
 +The multiplicative form is preferred over minimum functions because:                                                                                                                                                      
 +1. It's differentiable and numerically stable                                                                                                                                                                             
 +2. It approximates co-limitation observed in laboratory cultures                                                                                                                                                          
 +3. It's widely used in ocean biogeochemical models (e.g., NPZD models)                                                                                                                                                    
 +4. When one resource is very limiting, the product approaches that single limitation
