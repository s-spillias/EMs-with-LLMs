 2 Missing Ecological Process:                                                                                                                                                                                              
    • The model lacks light limitation for phytoplankton growth, which is a fundamental driver in oceanic mixed layer dynamics                                                                                              
    • Phytoplankton growth depends on both nutrients AND light availability                                                                                                                                                 
    • In the mixed layer, light varies with depth and can be a primary limiting factor, especially in deeper mixed layers or during winter

                                                                                                    Proposed Improvement                                                                                                    

I will add light limitation to phytoplankton growth using a multiplicative Monod-type term. This represents co-limitation by both nutrients and light, which is the standard approach in marine ecosystem models.           

Ecological Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                                   

 • In the oceanic mixed layer, light availability decreases with depth due to absorption and scattering                                                                                                                     
 • Phytoplankton growth is limited by whichever resource (light or nutrients) is most scarce                                                                                                                                
 • This is particularly important for capturing seasonal dynamics and bloom formation                                                                                                                                       
 • The improvement maintains model parsimony while adding critical realism                                                                                                                                                  

Mathematical Form:                                                                                                                                                                                                          

Growth will become: r_P * (N/(K_N + N)) * (I/(K_I + I)) * P                                                                                                                                                                 

Where:                                                                                                                                                                                                                      

 • I is light intensity (e.g., μmol photons m^-2 s^-1 or as a fraction of surface irradiance)                                                                                                                               
 • K_I is the half-saturation constant for light-limited growth
