Current Model Structure:                                                                                                                                                                                                    

The model implements a nutrient-phytoplankton-zooplankton (NPZ) system with:                                                                                                                                                

 • Light limitation with self-shading (Beer-Lambert law)                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Nutrient limitation (Monod kinetics)                                                                                                                                                                                     
 • Liebig's Law of the Minimum for phytoplankton growth                                                                                                                                                                     
 • Holling Type II grazing                                                                                                                                                                                                  
 • Linear + quadratic zooplankton mortality                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • Nutrient recycling                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Key Observation from Parameters:                                                                                                                                                                                            

Looking at the parameter values, I notice that epsilon_P = 0.8 (logit-transformed to ~0.69 or 69% efficiency) and epsilon_Z = -0.4 (logit-transformed to ~0.40 or 40% efficiency). These are reasonable values, but they're 
currently treated as constants.                                                                                                                                                                                             

Missing Ecological Process:                                                                                                                                                                                                 

A critical oversimplification is that nutrient uptake efficiency is constant. In reality, phytoplankton nutrient uptake efficiency varies with nutrient availability - at low nutrient concentrations, cells become more    
efficient at extracting nutrients (luxury uptake), while at high concentrations, efficiency may decrease due to saturation of uptake machinery. This is particularly important for the NPZ dynamics because it affects the  
coupling between nutrient depletion and phytoplankton growth.                                                                                                                                                               

Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Implement variable nutrient uptake efficiency that depends on nutrient availability. This will be represented as:                                                                                                           

 • At low N: higher efficiency (cells work harder to extract scarce nutrients)                                                                                                                                              
 • At high N: lower efficiency (uptake machinery saturates)                                                                                                                                                                 

This can be modeled with a decreasing function of nutrient concentration, using a simple inverse relationship that maintains biological realism.
