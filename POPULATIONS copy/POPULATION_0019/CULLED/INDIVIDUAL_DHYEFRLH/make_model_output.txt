 2 Missing Ecological Process:                                                                                                                                                                                              
   The most significant omission is light limitation for phytoplankton growth. In oceanic mixed layers, phytoplankton growth is co-limited by both nutrients AND light availability. The current model only considers       
   nutrient limitation, which oversimplifies the dynamics, especially in deeper mixed layers or during different seasons.                                                                                                   
 3 Ecological Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                                
   Light limitation is fundamental to phytoplankton ecology. In the mixed layer, light decreases exponentially with depth, and phytoplankton experience an average light level that depends on:                             
    • Surface irradiance (varies seasonally and daily)                                                                                                                                                                      
    • Mixed layer depth                                                                                                                                                                                                     
    • Self-shading by phytoplankton biomass                                                                                                                                                                                 
   This creates important feedback: high phytoplankton biomass reduces light availability (self-shading), which can limit further growth and contribute to bloom dynamics.                                                  

Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Add a light limitation term with self-shading effects using a multiplicative Monod-type formulation. This will make phytoplankton growth co-limited by both nutrients and light, which is more ecologically realistic for   
oceanic mixed layer dynamics.
