Proposed Improvement: Temperature-Dependent Metabolic Rates                                                                                                                                                                 

Temperature is one of the most important environmental drivers of plankton dynamics, affecting all biological rate processes through the Arrhenius relationship or Q10 formulation. This is particularly relevant for       
understanding seasonal cycles and climate change impacts on marine ecosystems.                                                                                                                                              

I'll implement temperature dependence using the Arrhenius equation, which is more mechanistically grounded than Q10:                                                                                                        

rate(T) = rate_ref × exp(-E_a/R × (1/T - 1/T_ref))                                                                                                                                                                          

Where:                                                                                                                                                                                                                      

 • E_a is activation energy                                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • R is the gas constant                                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • T is temperature (Kelvin)                                                                                                                                                                                                
 • T_ref is reference temperature                                                                                                                                                                                           

This will apply to: phytoplankton growth (r_P), zooplankton grazing (g_max), and mortality rates (m_P, m_Z).
