 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +Add light limitation with self-shading to phytoplankton growth using a multiplicative co-limitation framework:                                                                                                            
 +

 +- f(Nutrients) = N / (K_N + N)  [existing nutrient limitation]

 +

 +
