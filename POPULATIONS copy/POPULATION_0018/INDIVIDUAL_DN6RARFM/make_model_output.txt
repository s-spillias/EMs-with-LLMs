                                                                                                    Proposed Improvement                                                                                                    

I will add temperature-dependent rate modulation using a Q10 formulation. This is ecologically justified because:                                                                                                           

 • Phytoplankton growth rates typically double for every 10°C increase (Q10 ≈ 2)                                                                                                                                            
 • Zooplankton grazing and metabolism are similarly temperature-sensitive                                                                                                                                                   
 • This allows the model to capture seasonal dynamics driven by temperature variation                                                                                                                                       
 • It's a well-established relationship in marine ecology                                                                                                                                                                   

This is simpler than full Arrhenius kinetics but captures the essential temperature effect with fewer parameters.
