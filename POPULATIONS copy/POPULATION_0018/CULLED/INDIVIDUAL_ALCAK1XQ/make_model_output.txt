Current Model Structure:                                                                                                                                                                                                    

The model simulates a nutrient-phytoplankton-zooplankton (NPZ) system with:                                                                                                                                                 

 • Light limitation using Steele formulation with self-shading                                                                                                                                                              
 • Nutrient limitation via Michaelis-Menten kinetics                                                                                                                                                                        
 • Zooplankton grazing with Holling Type II functional response                                                                                                                                                             
 • Nutrient recycling from mortality                                                                                                                                                                                        
 • Linear and quadratic zooplankton mortality                                                                                                                                                                               

Key Observation:                                                                                                                                                                                                            

Looking at the parameter values, I notice that several efficiency and recycling parameters (epsilon=0.3, gamma_P=0.5, gamma_Z=0.3) suggest significant losses in energy transfer and nutrient cycling. However, these are   
currently treated as constants, which is ecologically unrealistic.                                                                                                                                                          

Missing Ecological Process:                                                                                                                                                                                                 

The model assumes constant assimilation efficiency (epsilon) regardless of food quality or quantity. In reality, zooplankton assimilation efficiency varies with:                                                           

 1 Food concentration - at low phytoplankton densities, zooplankton are less selective and may ingest non-food particles, reducing efficiency                                                                               
 2 Feeding rate - at very high feeding rates, gut passage time decreases, reducing digestion efficiency                                                                                                                     

This is a well-documented phenomenon in zooplankton ecology and can significantly affect energy transfer and nutrient cycling dynamics.                                                                                     


                                                                                                    Proposed Improvement                                                                                                    

Add variable zooplankton assimilation efficiency that depends on phytoplankton concentration and feeding rate. This will:                                                                                                   

 • Make energy transfer more realistic                                                                                                                                                                                      
 • Create feedback between phytoplankton abundance and zooplankton growth                                                                                                                                                   
 • Affect nutrient recycling (more unassimilated material = more recycled nutrients)                                                                                                                                        

I'll implement this using a saturating function where efficiency increases with phytoplankton concentration up to a maximum, representing improved feeding selectivity and digestion at higher food densities.
