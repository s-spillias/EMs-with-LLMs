light limitation of phytoplankton growth.                                                                                                                                                                                   

Missing Process:                                                                                                                                                                                                            

In the oceanic mixed layer, phytoplankton growth is co-limited by both nutrients AND light availability. The current model only considers nutrient limitation. Light availability varies with:                              

 1 Surface irradiance (seasonal/daily cycles)                                                                                                                                                                               
 2 Self-shading by phytoplankton (turbidity increases with phytoplankton biomass)                                                                                                                                           
 3 Mixed layer depth

Add light limitation using a multiplicative approach where phytoplankton growth is limited by the minimum of nutrient and light availability. I'll implement a self-shading formulation where light attenuation increases   
with phytoplankton biomass, creating a negative feedback that can stabilize oscillations and better represent mixed layer dynamics.                                                                                         

This is ecologically justified because:                                                                                                                                                                                     

 • Light is a fundamental resource for photosynthesis                                                                                                                                                                       
 • Self-shading is a well-documented density-dependent effect                                                                                                                                                               
 • It adds realism without excessive complexity                                                                                                                                                                             
 • It can help explain bloom dynamics and population regulation
