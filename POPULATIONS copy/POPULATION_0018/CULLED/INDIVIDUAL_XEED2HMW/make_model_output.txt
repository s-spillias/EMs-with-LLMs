                                                                                                 Missing Ecological Process                                                                                                 

A critical missing component is nutrient limitation of phytoplankton stoichiometry. The current model assumes:                                                                                                              

 • Phytoplankton have a fixed C:N ratio                                                                                                                                                                                     
 • Nutrient uptake exactly equals growth (1:1 carbon to nutrient ratio)

 • Under nutrient limitation: high C:N ratio (can exceed 15-20 mol C/mol N)                                                                                                                                                 

This affects:                                                                                                                                                                                                               

 1 Nutrient uptake efficiency: Nutrient-limited cells take up nutrients more efficiently relative to carbon fixation                                                                                                        
 2 Phytoplankton quality: Already partially captured via variable epsilon, but the stoichiometric mechanism should be explicit                                                                                              
 3 Nutrient recycling: The amount of nutrients locked in phytoplankton biomass varies                                                                                                                                       

                                                                                   Proposed Improvement: Variable Phytoplankton C:N Quota                                                                                   

Implement a dynamic phytoplankton C:N ratio that:                                                                                                                                                                           

 • Increases under nutrient limitation (carbon-rich, nutrient-poor cells)                                                                                                                                                   
 • Decreases under nutrient-replete conditions (balanced Redfield stoichiometry)                                                                                                                                            
 • Affects nutrient uptake rates and recycling dynamics                                                                                                                                                                     

This will decouple carbon fixation (growth) from nutrient uptake, making the model more mechanistically realistic.
