                                                                                  Proposed Improvement: Light Limitation with Self-Shading                                                                                  

For an oceanic mixed layer model, light limitation is critical. Phytoplankton growth should be limited by the minimum of nutrient and light availability (Liebig's Law of the Minimum). I'll implement:                     

 1 Light attenuation with depth using Beer-Lambert law                                                                                                                                                                      
 2 Self-shading where phytoplankton biomass reduces light penetration                                                                                                                                                       
 3 Average light availability across the mixed layer depth                                                                                                                                                                  
 4 Multiplicative limitation where both nutrient AND light constrain growth                                                                                                                                                 

This is ecologically justified because:                                                                                                                                                                                     

 • Oceanic mixed layers have finite depth with light decreasing exponentially                                                                                                                                               
 • Phytoplankton blooms create self-shading, limiting their own growth                                                                                                                                                      
 • This creates realistic boom-bust dynamics in plankton populations
