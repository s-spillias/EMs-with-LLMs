                                                                                                         Assessment                                                                                                         

Current Model Structure:                                                                                                                                                                                                    

The model implements a classic NPZ (Nutrient-Phytoplankton-Zooplankton) model with:                                                                                                                                         

 • Michaelis-Menten nutrient uptake by phytoplankton                                                                                                                                                                        
 • Holling Type II functional response for zooplankton grazing                                                                                                                                                              
 • Linear and quadratic zooplankton mortality                                                                                                                                                                               
 • Nutrient recycling from both phytoplankton and zooplankton mortality                                                                                                                                                     

Key Observation:                                                                                                                                                                                                            

Looking at the parameter values, I notice that the model assumes constant rates and efficiencies. However, in real oceanic systems, phytoplankton growth is often co-limited by both nutrients AND light availability, which
varies with depth and phytoplankton self-shading. The current model only considers nutrient limitation.                                                                                                                     

Missing Ecological Process:                                                                                                                                                                                                 

The model lacks light limitation and self-shading effects. In the oceanic mixed layer, phytoplankton growth is typically limited by the availability of both nutrients and light. As phytoplankton biomass increases, it    
reduces light penetration (self-shading), creating a negative feedback that can stabilize the system and produce more realistic bloom dynamics.                                                                             

Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Add a multiplicative light limitation term to phytoplankton growth that includes self-shading. This is ecologically justified because:                                                                                      

 1 Light is a fundamental requirement for photosynthesis                                                                                                                                                                    
 2 Self-shading is a well-documented density-dependent process                                                                                                                                                              
 3 It adds realism without excessive complexity                                                                                                                                                                             
 4 It can help stabilize oscillations and produce more realistic bloom patterns                                                                                                                                             

The light limitation will use a simple exponential decay model: I/I_0 * exp(-k_w * z - k_p * P) where light decreases with depth and phytoplankton concentration.
