Looking at the parameter values, I notice that several efficiency parameters (epsilon=0.3, gamma_P=0.5, gamma_Z=0.3) are fixed constants. In real oceanic systems, these efficiencies are often temperature-dependent and

nutrient export. This is particularly important for the PROJECT CONTEXT of plankton population dynamics in the oceanic mixed layer, as sinking can be a major loss mechanism comparable to or exceeding grazing in some     
conditions.

 1 Gravitational settling of phytoplankton cells out of the mixed layer
