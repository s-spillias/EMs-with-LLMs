Key Issue Identified:

Ecological Improvement: Temperature-Dependent Metabolic Rates                                                                                                                                                               

Temperature affects all biological rates in plankton systems. The Q10 formulation (rate doubles for every 10°C increase) is widely used in marine ecosystem models. This will make the model more realistic for:            

 • Seasonal plankton blooms (spring warming triggers growth)                                                                                                                                                                
 • Latitudinal gradients in productivity                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Climate change impacts on plankton dynamics                                                                                                                                                                              

I'll implement temperature dependence using the Q10 approach, which is simpler and more commonly used in NPZ models than Arrhenius kinetics.
