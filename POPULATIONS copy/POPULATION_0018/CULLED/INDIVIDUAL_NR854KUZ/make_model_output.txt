Missing Ecological Process:                                                                                                                                                                                                 

The model lacks a detritus pool for organic matter decomposition. Currently, dead phytoplankton and zooplankton are instantly converted to nutrients via gamma coefficients, but in reality:                                

 • Dead organic matter sinks and decomposes gradually

 • Detritus can be an important carbon export pathway

 • Sinking detritus exports carbon from the mixed layer                                                                                                                                                                     
 • Bacterial decomposition rates control nutrient regeneration timing                                                                                                                                                       
 • The balance between recycling and export affects bloom dynamics
