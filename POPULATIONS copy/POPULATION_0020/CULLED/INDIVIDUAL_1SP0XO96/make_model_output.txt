   zooplankton population (Z) increases, the mortality rate increases quadratically, reflecting a more efficient or aggregated predatory response. This is a common and critical feature for stabilizing plankton models,   
   preventing the zooplankton from overgrazing the phytoplankton and causing population crashes.
