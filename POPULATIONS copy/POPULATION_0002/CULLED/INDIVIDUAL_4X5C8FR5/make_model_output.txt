 2 Explanation of changes                                                                                                                                                                                                                               

 • Ecological addition: Juvenile-to-adult maturation is multiplied by a refuge index derived from total coral cover (A+S). Ecological rationale: Juvenile COTS benefit from coral structure (shelter and foraging habitat). After outbreaks, coral loss 
   reduces juvenile survival to adulthood, reinforcing the crash and delaying the next boom until coral recovers. This is a parsimonious mechanism aligned with observed dynamics and does not introduce data leakage (uses t-1 coral states).
