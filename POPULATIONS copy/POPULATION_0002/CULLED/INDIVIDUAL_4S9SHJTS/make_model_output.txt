adults after one year, which compresses the temporal dynamics and can under-represent outbreak lag and sustainment.                                                                                                                                     

 2 Explanation of needed changes                                                                                                                                                                                                                        

 • Structural improvement: Add an unobserved juvenile state J_t. Larvae plus immigration feed into juveniles; a fraction of juveniles mature to adults each year. This introduces a realistic multi-year delay that helps generate and sustain boom-bust
   cycles.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
 • Equations:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
    • J_{t+1} = sJ * J_t + L_t                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
    • C_{t+1} = sA_t * C_t + mu_adult * J_t                                                                                                                                                                                                             
   where sJ = exp(-mJ) with mJ > 0, and L_t remains the same larval production + immigration term.
