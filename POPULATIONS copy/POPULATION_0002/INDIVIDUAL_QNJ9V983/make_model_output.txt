In this new structure:

 CHOICE OF ONE MEANINGFUL ECOLOGICAL IMPROVEMENT                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 Improvement: Replace the predation term with a multi-prey Holling Type III functional response that is prey-dependent, saturates via a shared handling time, and scales with COTS density. This better captures:                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Rapid coral loss during outbreak peaks (predation scales with C).                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Prey switching and non-linear prey-dependence (Type III via Hill exponent).                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Saturation of feeding at high prey availability (handling time).                                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Stronger feedbacks: heavy coral depletion reduces future COTS growth via reduced food_avail and increased starvation mortality.                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 Mathematical form implemented                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Let A_prop and S_prop be previous-year coral covers (proportions). Define prey availability with Hill exponent ν_FR:                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
   A_h = (A_prop + ε)^(ν_FR), S_h = (S_prop + ε)^(ν_FR).                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Multi-prey Holling with shared handling time h_FR:                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
   den_FR = 1 + h_FR*(c_attack_A*A_h + c_attack_S*S_h)                                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
   fA_per_pred = (c_attack_A*A_h)/den_FR                                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
   fS_per_pred = (c_attack_S*S_h)/den_FR                                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Convert per-predator hazard to a bounded annual proportion loss using a hazard-to-probability transform:                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
   pred_A_prop = 1 - exp(-C_prev * fA_per_pred)                                                                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
   pred_S_prop = 1 - exp(-C_prev * fS_per_pred)                                                                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - This replaces the previous predation term pred_*_prop = c_attack_* * H(C_prev) * *_prop, which approached a predator-independent ceiling at high C.                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 Expected effects on dynamics and fit                                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Outbreak peaks: Higher predation on Acropora and then on massive corals as C increases and prey are available; sharper boom dynamics.                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Bust transition: Faster collapse once corals are depleted due to stronger coupling of predation to both prey and predator density, reinforcing starvation feedback.                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Selectivity preserved: Distinct c_attack_A vs. c_attack_S; ν_FR controls switching strength.                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
 - Improved realism without introducing stage structure or many new parameters.
