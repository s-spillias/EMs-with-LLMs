I understand the request.                                                                                                                                                                                                                               

 1 Are code changes needed?                                                                                                                                                                                                                             

Yes. To better capture outbreak timing and duration without data leakage and within the available forcings, I propose one focused ecological improvement: replace the fixed two-point lag mixture for larval immigration with a flexible, ecologically  
grounded exponential lag kernel over 0–2 years. This represents the juvenile cryptic stage and maturation delay (≈2–3 years), a key mechanism underpinning boom timing.                                                                                 

 2 Explanation of the changes                                                                                                                                                                                                                           

 • Rationale: The current model uses a single parameter (rho_lag_I) to split immigration between t−1 and t−2. Outbreaks are often driven by larval pulses that manifest in adult observations after a variable delay due to juvenile maturation. A      
   short/rigid lag can create phase errors in peaks and troughs. An exponential kernel over 0–2 years adds necessary flexibility with just one parameter (tau_lag_I), maintaining parsimony while better aligning peak timing and duration with observed
   boom-bust cycles.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • What changes:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
    • Replace rho_lag_I with tau_lag_I (years), controlling weights across 0-, 1-, and 2-year lags via a normalized exponential kernel.                                                                                                                 
    • Update immigration term to immig_eff = gamma_I * sum_k w_k * cotsimm_{t-k-1} for k=0..2 with robust boundary handling for the first two steps.                                                                                                    
    • Add parameter bounds and penalties for tau_lag_I; remove penalties for rho_lag_I.                                                                                                                                                                 
    • Document the assessment and reasoning in intention.txt.                                                                                                                                                                                           
    • Update parameters.json accordingly.
