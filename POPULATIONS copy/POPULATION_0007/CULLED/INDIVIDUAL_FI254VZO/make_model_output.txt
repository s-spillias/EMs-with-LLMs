I understand the request and will make one ecologically meaningful improvement focused on juvenile recruitment realism:                                                                                                         

 1 Are code changes needed?                                                                                                                                                                                                     

Yes. The current recruitment success depends positively on coral cover (via a weighted resource index). This likely suppresses outbreak initiation when coral cover is low, whereas COTS early juveniles benefit from open      
substrate and algal films following coral loss. To better capture boom-bust dynamics, I will replace the coral-based resource index with an open-substrate-mediated modifier for juvenile recruitment.                          

 2 Explanation of changes                                                                                                                                                                                                       

 • Ecological rationale: Early juvenile COTS rely on algal films and shelter on open substrate; high coral cover reduces such habitat. After coral loss (bleaching or predation), open substrate increases, potentially         
   improving juvenile survival/settlement and seeding the next outbreak. Introducing a saturating function of open substrate into recruitment adds a realistic indirect feedback and helps capture episodic outbreaks.          
 • Mathematical change in model.cpp:                                                                                                                                                                                            
    • Replace f_food = R/(K_R + R) with f_sub = O/(K_sub + O), where O = max(0, 100 - (F + S)).                                                                                                                                 
    • Use f_sub in the recruitment equation Rec = alpha_rec * C^phi * f_Allee * f_sub * f_Trec + immigration.                                                                                                                   
    • Remove parameters K_R, wF, wS. Add K_sub.
