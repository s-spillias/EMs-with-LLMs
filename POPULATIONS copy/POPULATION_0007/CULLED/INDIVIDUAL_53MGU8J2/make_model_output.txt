   limitation (i.e., the NP part of NPZ). A saturating food limitation is added to recruitment to reflect that larval survival depends on       
   phytoplankton availability, which is often higher under cooler, more mixed conditions. This is implemented as a function of SST using a      
   simple, identifiable form that avoids introducing latent stochastic states or new data requirements.

   drivers (SST, immigration).
