Summary of assessment                                                                                                                                                                                                           

 • Fit to data: Residuals file not present; please share it to refine diagnostics. Code-wise, immediate recruitment to adults likely causes phase mismatch: peaks may occur too early relative to observed outbreaks, and       
   declines may be too fast/slow depending on gammaC. A lagged pathway generally improves timing and can generate realistic oscillations.                                                                                       
 • Alignment with project context: The model includes Allee effects, resource-limited recruitment, temperature effects on recruitment and bleaching, multi-prey functional response, and shared space—good coverage. However, it
   lacks an explicit juvenile stage and associated delay between spawning and adult abundance, a key mechanism for episodic outbreaks.                                                                                          
 • Missing/oversimplified processes:                                                                                                                                                                                            
    • No juvenile stage/maturation delay from larval settlement to adults.                                                                                                                                                      
    • Immigration is exogenous but fine for forcing; could be left as-is.                                                                                                                                                       
    • Observation model is adequate; no change required.                                                                                                                                                                        
    • Type-III elements (etaF/S ≥ 1) are present and help low-cover refuge.                                                                                                                                                     

Chosen ecological improvement                                                                                                                                                                                                   

 • Add a juvenile COTS compartment J with annual maturation mJ into adults and juvenile mortality muJ. Recruitment now fills the juvenile pool; adults increase via mJ*J, and experience mortality as before. This introduces a 
   biologically realistic lag that helps capture outbreak timing and persistence.                                                                                                                                               
 • New parameters:                                                                                                                                                                                                              
    • mJ (yr^-1): annual maturation fraction of juveniles becoming adults (0–1).                                                                                                                                                
    • muJ (yr^-1): annual proportional juvenile mortality (0–1).                                                                                                                                                                
 • Changes to equations:                                                                                                                                                                                                        
    • Rec_t-1 as before, but into J:                                                                                                                                                                                            
      J_t = J_{t-1} + Rec_{t-1} - (mJ + muJ) J_{t-1}                                                                                                                                                                            
    • Adults:                                                                                                                                                                                                                   
      C_t = C_{t-1} + mJ J_{t-1} - (muC + gammaC C_{t-1}) C_{t-1}                                                                                                                                                               
    • No current-time observations used in process updates; all drivers are t-1.
