                                                                                                    Key Missing Process                                                                                                     

The most critical missing element is stage-structured population dynamics for COTS. The episodic nature of outbreaks requires:                                                                                              

 • Larval settlement events (immigration) creating cohorts                                                                                                                                                                  
 • Juvenile development period before reproduction                                                                                                                                                                          
 • Adult reproduction and mortality                                                                                                                                                                                         

However, adding full age structure would be very complex. A simpler improvement that captures the essential dynamics is to modify the recruitment function to include a larval settlement success term that depends on      
environmental conditions and creates the pulsed recruitment pattern characteristic of outbreaks.                                                                                                                            

                                                                                                    Proposed Improvement                                                                                                    

I'll implement a larval settlement success function that modulates immigration based on:                                                                                                                                    

 1 Substrate availability (space not occupied by coral or other COTS)                                                                                                                                                       
 2 Temperature suitability (already have temp_opt)                                                                                                                                                                          
 3 Conspecific attraction (low-density Allee effect for settlement, not just reproduction)                                                                                                                                  

This creates a mechanism where larval pulses (immigration) can trigger outbreaks when conditions are right, but fail when conditions are poor - generating the episodic pattern.                                            

I'll also separate local recruitment (from existing population) from larval settlement (from immigration) to better represent the two sources of population increase.
