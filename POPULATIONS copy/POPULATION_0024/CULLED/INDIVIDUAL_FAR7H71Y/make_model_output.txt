 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +Add nutrient-enhanced recruitment mechanism:

 +   - Smooth, differentiable function for optimization                                                                                                                                                                     
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +4. Integration into COTS growth (Equation 11):                                                                                                                                                                            
 +   Multiply the recruitment term by nutrient_enhancement
