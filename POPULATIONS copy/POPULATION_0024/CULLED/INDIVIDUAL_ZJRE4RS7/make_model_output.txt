                                                                                                  Ecological Justification                                                                                                  

COTS larvae show:                                                                                                                                                                                                           

 1 Asymmetric thermal performance: Cold temperatures are more limiting than warm temperatures                                                                                                                               
 2 Minimum temperature threshold: Below ~26°C, recruitment essentially fails                                                                                                                                                
 3 Broad upper tolerance: Can recruit successfully across a wider range above optimum                                                                                                                                       

This asymmetry is crucial for episodic outbreaks because:                                                                                                                                                                   

 • It creates sharper recruitment windows                                                                                                                                                                                   
 • It makes outbreaks more sensitive to specific thermal conditions                                                                                                                                                         
 • It better represents the biological reality of larval development                                                                                                                                                        


                                                                                                   Mathematical Approach                                                                                                    

Replace the symmetric Gaussian (Equation 2) with an asymmetric beta-like function that:                                                                                                                                     

 • Has a steep decline below optimal temperature                                                                                                                                                                            
 • Has a gentler decline above optimal temperature                                                                                                                                                                          
 • Includes a hard lower threshold                                                                                                                                                                                          
 • Uses biologically meaningful parameters                                                                                                                                                                                  

This adds minimal complexity (one parameter) but significantly improves ecological realism given the updated temperature optimum from literature.
