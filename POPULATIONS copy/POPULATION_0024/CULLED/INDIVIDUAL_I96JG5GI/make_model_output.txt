The model addresses key aspects:                                                                                                                                                                                            

 • ✓ Boom-bust cycles through outbreak amplification mechanism                                                                                                                                                              
 • ✓ Selective predation on different coral types                                                                                                                                                                           
 • ✓ Outbreak triggers (temperature, immigration, food availability)                                                                                                                                                        

However, there are potential issues:                                                                                                                                                                                        

 • The outbreak mechanism (Equations 11-14) uses a geometric mean of three favorability indices, which may be too restrictive - all three conditions must be simultaneously favorable                                       
 • The outbreak amplification is applied uniformly to all growth terms, which may not reflect the biological reality that outbreaks are primarily driven by recruitment pulses rather than adult growth enhancement         


                                                                                           3. Missing or Oversimplified Processes                                                                                           

Critical Issue Identified: The model treats outbreak amplification as a multiplier on the entire growth equation, but ecological evidence suggests COTS outbreaks are primarily driven by episodic recruitment events when  
larvae settle en masse under favorable conditions. The current structure (Equation 14) amplifies both:                                                                                                                      

 1 Adult population growth (logistic term)                                                                                                                                                                                  
 2 Conversion from predation                                                                                                                                                                                                

This is ecologically unrealistic because:                                                                                                                                                                                   

 • Outbreaks start from larval recruitment, not from existing adults suddenly growing faster                                                                                                                                
 • The conversion efficiency shouldn't change during outbreaks                                                                                                                                                              
 • The current mechanism could create outbreaks even without favorable recruitment conditions                                                                                                                               


                                                                                                 4. Parameter Value Review                                                                                                  

Examining log_temp_opt (updated from literature to 3.349, ~28°C) - this is appropriate and the equation structure handles it well. Other parameters appear reasonable for their current equation forms.                     


                                                                                 Proposed Improvement: Separate Recruitment Pulse Mechanism                                                                                 

Ecological Rationale:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

COTS outbreaks are fundamentally recruitment-driven events. When conditions align (warm temperatures, high food availability, larval supply), massive cohorts of juveniles settle and survive. This is distinct from adult  
population processes.                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Mathematical Approach:                                                                                                                                                                                                      

Replace the uniform outbreak amplifier with a dedicated recruitment pulse term that:                                                                                                                                        

 1 Activates only when conditions favor larval settlement and early survival                                                                                                                                                
 2 Adds new individuals through a separate recruitment pathway                                                                                                                                                              
 3 Uses a multiplicative (not geometric mean) favorability index to allow partial compensation among factors                                                                                                                
 4 Includes a threshold-based activation to capture the episodic nature                                                                                                                                                     

This better captures the ecological reality that outbreaks begin with recruitment events, not enhanced adult growth.
