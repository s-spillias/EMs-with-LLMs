                                                                                                         Assessment                                                                                                         

                                                                                                 Current Model Performance                                                                                                  

Looking at the model structure, I can identify several strengths and a critical weakness:                                                                                                                                   

Strengths:                                                                                                                                                                                                                  

 • Comprehensive COTS population dynamics with Allee effects, temperature dependence, and density-dependent mortality                                                                                                       
 • Two coral functional groups (fast/slow growing) with differential predation                                                                                                                                              
 • Type II functional responses for predation                                                                                                                                                                               
 • Recruitment pulse mechanism with composite favorability index                                                                                                                                                            

Critical Issue:                                                                                                                                                                                                             

The recruitment pulse mechanism (Equations 11-18) has a fundamental problem. The substrate_food_favorability uses a unimodal relationship that peaks at optimal_coral (~30%), but this creates a paradox:                   

 1 During COTS outbreaks, coral cover drops dramatically (often to <10%)                                                                                                                                                    
 2 At low coral cover, substrate_food_favorability becomes very small                                                                                                                                                       
 3 This prevents the outbreak from sustaining itself through continued recruitment                                                                                                                                          
 4 Yet real COTS outbreaks persist for 2-3 years even as they devastate coral                                                                                                                                               

The current formulation assumes larvae need intermediate coral cover for settlement substrate AND food. However, ecological evidence suggests:                                                                              

 • Larval settlement is primarily on crustose coralline algae (CCA), which is MORE available when coral cover is LOW                                                                                                        
 • Post-settlement food requirements are minimal - juvenile COTS can survive on biofilm, detritus, and small coral fragments                                                                                                
 • Adult food limitation only matters later, causing outbreak collapse                                                                                                                                                      

                                                                                                 Missing Ecological Process                                                                                                 

The model lacks a temporal decoupling between:                                                                                                                                                                              

 1 Settlement phase (favored by LOW coral = high CCA availability)                                                                                                                                                          
 2 Growth/reproduction phase (requires adequate coral food for adults)                                                                                                                                                      

Real outbreaks show a characteristic pattern:                                                                                                                                                                               

 • Initial recruitment occurs during coral recovery (moderate cover)                                                                                                                                                        
 • Once established, COTS continue recruiting even as they reduce coral cover                                                                                                                                               
 • Outbreak persists until coral is so depleted that adult starvation/mortality dominates                                                                                                                                   


                                                                                                    Proposed Improvement                                                                                                    

I will separate settlement favorability from food availability in the recruitment pulse mechanism:                                                                                                                          

 1 Settlement favorability: Inverse relationship with coral cover (more CCA when coral is low)                                                                                                                              
 2 Juvenile survival: Minimal coral requirement (juveniles are generalists)                                                                                                                                                 
 3 Adult reproduction: Depends on adequate food (current coral levels)                                                                                                                                                      

This creates a more realistic outbreak dynamic where:                                                                                                                                                                       

 • Recruitment can continue even as coral declines                                                                                                                                                                          
 • Outbreaks are self-limiting only when coral becomes critically depleted                                                                                                                                                  
 • The boom-bust cycle emerges naturally from the predator-prey dynamics
