                                                                                                    Proposed Improvement                                                                                                    

Implement the nutrient-mediated larval survival mechanism using a Michaelis-Menten (hyperbolic) response. This is the most critical missing piece for modeling episodic outbreaks.                                          

Ecological Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                                   

 • COTS larvae feed on phytoplankton during their ~2-3 week pelagic phase                                                                                                                                                   
 • Nutrient pulses (especially from terrestrial runoff) cause phytoplankton blooms                                                                                                                                          
 • Higher phytoplankton availability dramatically increases larval survival from <1% to potentially 10-20%                                                                                                                  
 • This creates the episodic trigger for outbreaks when nutrient conditions align with spawning events                                                                                                                      
 • The Michaelis-Menten form captures saturation: beyond a certain nutrient level, additional nutrients don't proportionally increase survival                                                                              

Mathematical Form:                                                                                                                                                                                                          

                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
 nutrient_effect = nutrient_curr / (nutrient_half_sat + nutrient_curr)                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            

This provides:                                                                                                                                                                                                              

 • 0.5 × max effect at nutrient_half_sat                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Asymptotic approach to maximum (1.0) at high nutrients                                                                                                                                                                   
 • Near-zero effect when nutrients are very low                                                                                                                                                                             
 • Ecologically realistic saturating response
