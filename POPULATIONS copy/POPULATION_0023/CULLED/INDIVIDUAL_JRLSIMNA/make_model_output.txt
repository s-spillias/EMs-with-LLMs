                                                                                                1. Current Model Evaluation                                                                                                 

The model includes:                                                                                                                                                                                                         

 • Age-structured COTS population (juveniles and adults) - good for capturing demographic dynamics                                                                                                                          
 • Two coral types (fast and slow growing) with differential predation                                                                                                                                                      
 • Temperature effects on recruitment via Gaussian response                                                                                                                                                                 
 • Allee effects on adult reproduction                                                                                                                                                                                      
 • Nutrient effects on larval survival with outbreak threshold mechanism                                                                                                                                                    
 • Type II functional responses for feeding                                                                                                                                                                                 


                                                                                             2. Alignment with PROJECT CONTEXT                                                                                              

Strengths:                                                                                                                                                                                                                  

 • Age structure allows for delayed outbreak dynamics                                                                                                                                                                       
 • Nutrient outbreak threshold mechanism attempts to capture episodic nature                                                                                                                                                
 • Differential coral predation is included                                                                                                                                                                                 

Potential Issues:                                                                                                                                                                                                           

 • The current nutrient forcing defaults to 1.0 (baseline) when not provided, which may not capture episodic outbreak triggers                                                                                              
 • The larval survival mechanism combines local reproduction and immigration, but the conversion from adult reproduction to juvenile recruitment may be oversimplified                                                      
 • The model lacks explicit representation of the lag between favorable conditions and observable outbreaks - a key feature of COTS boom-bust cycles                                                                        


                                                                                        3. Key Missing Process: Larval Pool Dynamics                                                                                        

Looking at the current structure, I notice that larval recruitment is instantaneous - favorable conditions (temperature, nutrients, immigration) immediately produce juveniles in the same time step. However, COTS ecology 
involves:                                                                                                                                                                                                                   

 1 Larval development period (~2-4 weeks in plankton)                                                                                                                                                                       
 2 Settlement cues and habitat requirements                                                                                                                                                                                 
 3 Cumulative effects of favorable conditions over multiple seasons                                                                                                                                                         

The episodic nature of COTS outbreaks often results from multiple consecutive years of favorable recruitment conditions, not just a single year. This creates a "memory" effect where the larval pool accumulates over time.


                                                                                   Proposed Improvement: Explicit Larval Pool Compartment                                                                                   

I propose adding a larval pool state variable that:                                                                                                                                                                         

 • Accumulates larvae from adult reproduction and immigration                                                                                                                                                               
 • Experiences mortality during the planktonic phase                                                                                                                                                                        
 • Settles to become juveniles based on environmental conditions and available settlement habitat                                                                                                                           
 • Creates temporal lag between spawning and recruitment                                                                                                                                                                    

This addresses the PROJECT CONTEXT by:                                                                                                                                                                                      

 • Capturing outbreak timing: Multiple favorable years build up larval pool before mass settlement                                                                                                                          
 • Explaining magnitude: Accumulated larvae can produce explosive recruitment events                                                                                                                                        
 • Creating boom-bust dynamics: Larval pool depletes after settlement, requiring rebuilding                                                                                                                                 

The mathematical implementation will use:                                                                                                                                                                                   

 • Larval pool accumulation: Production from adults + immigration - larval mortality - settlement                                                                                                                           
 • Settlement rate: Function of larval density, coral availability (settlement substrate), and environmental conditions                                                                                                     
 • Larval mortality: Higher than juvenile/adult mortality (realistic for planktonic stage)
