 1 Are code changes needed?                                                                                                                     

Yes. I introduce separate temperature sensitivities for phytoplankton growth and zooplankton grazing. The current model applies a single Q10 to 
both phytoplankton growth and zooplankton ingestion, which can force them to respond identically to temperature. Ecologically, these processes  
often have different temperature dependencies; allowing them to differ can improve seasonal phase and amplitude matching between P and Z.
