Yes. Based on the PROJECT CONTEXT and a structural assessment of the model, a key ecological process is missing: dilution/export of             
phytoplankton and zooplankton by vertical mixing. Currently, only the nutrient pool N is coupled to the deep reservoir via mixing, while P and Z
are not. In a mixed-layer one-box model, vertical exchange dilutes all tracers in the box, not only nutrients. Adding symmetric mixing terms for
P and Z is a meaningful, physically grounded improvement that typically reduces unrealistically sharp or persistent blooms, adjusts phase lags  
between P and Z, and improves ecological realism without introducing new parameters.
