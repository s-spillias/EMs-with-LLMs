Key Observation:

Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Add photoinhibition to the light limitation term. At very high light intensities (especially near the surface), phytoplankton can experience photodamage that reduces growth rates. This is particularly important in       
shallow mixed layers or high-light conditions. The current model uses a simple Michaelis-Menten saturation curve for light, but a more realistic formulation would include both light limitation at low intensities AND     
photoinhibition at high intensities.                                                                                                                                                                                        

I'll implement a Steele (1962) or Platt et al. (1980) photosynthesis-irradiance curve that captures both light limitation and photoinhibition. This uses an exponential formulation that peaks at an optimal light intensity
and decreases at higher intensities.
