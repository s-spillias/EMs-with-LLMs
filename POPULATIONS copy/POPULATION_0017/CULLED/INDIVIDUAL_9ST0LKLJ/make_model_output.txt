Current Model Structure:                                                                                                                                                                                                    

The model implements a classic NPZ (Nutrient-Phytoplankton-Zooplankton) model with:                                                                                                                                         

 • Michaelis-Menten nutrient uptake by phytoplankton                                                                                                                                                                        
 • Holling Type II functional response for zooplankton grazing                                                                                                                                                              
 • Linear phytoplankton mortality                                                                                                                                                                                           
 • Linear + quadratic zooplankton mortality                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • Nutrient recycling from mortality and inefficient grazing                                                                                                                                                                

Key Observation:                                                                                                                                                                                                            

Without residuals data, I'm analyzing the model structure itself. The current model assumes constant maximum rates (V_max, g_max) regardless of environmental conditions. In oceanic mixed layers, light availability is a  
critical limiting factor for phytoplankton growth that varies with depth, season, and turbidity. The model currently only considers nutrient limitation.                                                                    

Ecological Gap:                                                                                                                                                                                                             

Phytoplankton in the oceanic mixed layer experience co-limitation by both nutrients AND light. The current model only accounts for nutrient limitation through the Michaelis-Menten term. This oversimplification can lead  
to:                                                                                                                                                                                                                         

 • Overestimation of phytoplankton growth when nutrients are available but light is limiting                                                                                                                                
 • Poor representation of seasonal dynamics                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • Inability to capture depth-dependent productivity patterns                                                                                                                                                               

Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Implement multiplicative co-limitation where phytoplankton growth is limited by the product of nutrient and light limitation terms. This is a well-established approach in marine ecosystem modeling (e.g., Fasham et al.   
1990, Evans & Parslow 1985).                                                                                                                                                                                                

The modification changes:                                                                                                                                                                                                   

                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
 uptake = V_max * (N / (K_N + N)) * P                                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            

To:                                                                                                                                                                                                                         

                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
 uptake = V_max * (N / (K_N + N)) * (I / (K_I + I)) * P                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            

Where I is light intensity and K_I is the half-saturation constant for light limitation.                                                                                                                                    

For simplicity in a mixed layer model, we can use a constant average light intensity parameter, representing the mean light availability in the mixed layer.
