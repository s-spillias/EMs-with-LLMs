 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +### Identified Gap:                                                                                                                                                                                                       
 +The current model uses instantaneous Michaelis-Menten nutrient limitation, where growth is directly coupled to external nutrient concentration. This oversimplifies real phytoplankton physiology, which includes:        
 +- Internal nutrient storage (luxury uptake)                                                                                                                                                                               
 +- Decoupling of nutrient uptake from growth                                                                                                                                                                               
 +- Variable cellular nutrient quotas                                                                                                                                                                                       
 +- Ability to grow temporarily on stored nutrients when external nutrients are depleted                                                                                                                                    
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +This gap is particularly important for explaining:                                                                                                                                                                        
 +- Phytoplankton blooms that persist after external nutrient depletion                                                                                                                                                     
 +- The lag between nutrient pulses and growth responses                                                                                                                                                                    
 +- Variable C:N ratios in phytoplankton biomass                                                                                                                                                                            
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +## Proposed Ecological Improvement: Internal Nutrient Quota Model                                                                                                                                                         
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +### Ecological Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                             
 +Implement a quota-based growth model (Droop 1968, 1973) where phytoplankton maintain an internal nutrient pool (Q, units: g nutrient / g phytoplankton C) that varies between:                                            
 +- Q_min: minimum quota needed for survival (subsistence quota)                                                                                                                                                            
 +- Q_max: maximum quota (storage capacity)                                                                                                                                                                                 
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +### Mathematical Framework:                                                                                                                                                                                               
 +1. **Nutrient uptake** (V): Depends on external nutrients and current quota                                                                                                                                               
 +   - V = V_max * (N / (K_N + N)) * ((Q_max - Q) / (Q_max - Q_min))                                                                                                                                                        
 +   - Uptake decreases as internal quota approaches maximum (down-regulation)                                                                                                                                              
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +2. **Growth rate**: Depends on internal quota, not external nutrients                                                                                                                                                     
 +   - μ = r * (1 - Q_min/Q) * light_limitation                                                                                                                                                                             
 +   - Growth approaches maximum (r) when quota is high                                                                                                                                                                     
 +   - Growth approaches zero when quota approaches minimum                                                                                                                                                                 
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +3. **State variables**: Add internal quota Q as a new state variable                                                                                                                                                      
 +   - dQ/dt = V - μ*Q (quota changes via uptake minus dilution by growth)                                                                                                                                                  
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +### New Parameters Required:                                                                                                                                                                                              
 +- **V_max**: Maximum nutrient uptake rate (day^-1)                                                                                                                                                                        
 +  - Ecological meaning: Maximum rate of nutrient transport into cells                                                                                                                                                     
 +  - Expected range: 1.5-3.0 day^-1 (typically higher than growth rate)                                                                                                                                                    
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +- **Q_min**: Minimum (subsistence) nutrient quota (g nutrient / g C)                                                                                                                                                      
 +  - Ecological meaning: Minimum cellular nutrient content for survival                                                                                                                                                    
 +  - Expected range: 0.05-0.15 (based on Redfield ratio ~0.15 for N:C)                                                                                                                                                     
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +- **Q_max**: Maximum (storage) nutrient quota (g nutrient / g C)                                                                                                                                                          
 +  - Ecological meaning: Maximum cellular nutrient storage capacity                                                                                                                                                        
 +  - Expected range: 0.2-0.5 (2-3× minimum quota)                                                                                                                                                                          
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +### Modified Parameters:                                                                                                                                                                                                  
 +- **K_N**: Now applies to uptake rather than growth                                                                                                                                                                       
 +  - Retains same ecological meaning and range                                                                                                                                                                             
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +- **r**: Now represents maximum growth rate at optimal quota                                                                                                                                                              
 +  - Retains current value (1.0 day^-1) from literature                                                                                                                                                                    
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +### Expected Improvements:                                                                                                                                                                                                
 +1. **Bloom dynamics**: Phytoplankton can bloom rapidly using stored nutrients even as external N depletes                                                                                                                 
 +2. **Nutrient pulses**: Model captures delayed growth response to nutrient inputs                                                                                                                                         
 +3. **Seasonal patterns**: Better representation of spring blooms with luxury uptake                                                                                                                                       
 +4. **Ecological realism**: Aligns with known phytoplankton physiology                                                                                                                                                     
 +5. **Model fit**: Should reduce residuals during bloom periods where simple Monod fails                                                                                                                                   
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +### Implementation Notes:                                                                                                                                                                                                 
 +- Initial quota (Q_0) will be set to intermediate value (e.g., 0.2)                                                                                                                                                       
 +- Quota is a property of phytoplankton biomass, so total internal nutrient = Q * P                                                                                                                                        
 +- Nutrient uptake removes from external pool: dN/dt includes -V*P term                                                                                                                                                    
 +- Growth dilutes quota: dQ/dt includes -μ*Q term                                                                                                                                                                          
 +- All existing temperature and light controls remain intact                                                                                                                                                               
 +                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
 +This improvement maintains the model's existing strengths while adding a well-established ecological mechanism that addresses the identified gap in nutrient-growth coupling.
