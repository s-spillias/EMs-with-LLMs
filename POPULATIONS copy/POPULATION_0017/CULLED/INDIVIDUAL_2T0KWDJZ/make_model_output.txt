                                                                                   Proposed Improvement: Phytoplankton Growth Saturation                                                                                    

The most impactful improvement would be to add phytoplankton self-limitation through intraspecific competition. Currently, growth is only limited by nutrients and light externally, but high phytoplankton densities can   
cause:                                                                                                                                                                                                                      

 • Increased self-shading (already modeled)                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • Allelopathy (chemical inhibition)                                                                                                                                                                                        
 • Space/resource competition beyond simple nutrient depletion                                                                                                                                                              
 • Increased viral lysis at high densities                                                                                                                                                                                  

I propose adding a carrying capacity term to phytoplankton growth that creates a smooth saturation effect. This is ecologically justified because:                                                                          

 1 The high growth rate (r = 1.0 day^-1) from literature suggests rapid growth potential                                                                                                                                    
 2 Real phytoplankton blooms show self-limitation even with nutrients available                                                                                                                                             
 3 This prevents unrealistic exponential growth when conditions are favorable                                                                                                                                               

The mathematical form will be: (1 - P/K_P_max) where K_P_max is the phytoplankton carrying capacity, creating logistic-like growth limitation.
