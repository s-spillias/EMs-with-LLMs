Looking at the model structure, this is a comprehensive NPZ (Nutrient-Phytoplankton-Zooplankton) model with:

                                                                                              Key Observation from Parameters                                                                                               

The parameter r (phytoplankton growth rate) has been updated from literature with a value of 1.0 day^-1 and bounds [0.8, 1.2]. This is relatively high and suggests the model may need better representation of nutrient    
storage and luxury uptake in phytoplankton.                                                                                                                                                                                 

                                                                                                 Missing Ecological Process                                                                                                 

The current model assumes instantaneous coupling between nutrient uptake and growth (Droop-like quota dynamics are absent). In reality, phytoplankton can:                                                                  

 1 Take up nutrients in excess of immediate growth needs (luxury uptake)                                                                                                                                                    
 2 Store nutrients internally as cell quota                                                                                                                                                                                 
 3 Continue growing even when external nutrients are depleted, using internal stores                                                                                                                                        

This is particularly important for explaining:                                                                                                                                                                              

 • Phytoplankton blooms that persist after nutrient depletion                                                                                                                                                               
 • Time lags between nutrient availability and growth responses                                                                                                                                                             
 • More realistic bloom dynamics                                                                                                                                                                                            

                                                                         Proposed Improvement: Phytoplankton Internal Nutrient Quota (Droop Model)                                                                          

I will implement a variable internal nutrient quota model following Droop (1968, 1973). This adds one state variable (phytoplankton internal quota Q) and modifies the growth formulation to depend on internal nutrient    
status rather than external nutrient concentration alone.                                                                                                                                                                   

Ecological justification:                                                                                                                                                                                                   

 • Decouples nutrient uptake from growth, allowing luxury uptake                                                                                                                                                            
 • Growth rate depends on internal quota: μ = μ_max * (1 - Q_min/Q)                                                                                                                                                         
 • Nutrient uptake follows Michaelis-Menten kinetics but is independent of growth                                                                                                                                           
 • More realistic representation of phytoplankton physiology                                                                                                                                                                
 • Better captures bloom dynamics and nutrient-phytoplankton phase relationships
