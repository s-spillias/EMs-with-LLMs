                                                                                                         Assessment                                                                                                         

Current Model Structure:                                                                                                                                                                                                    

The model simulates NPZ dynamics with:                                                                                                                                                                                      

 • Nutrient uptake by phytoplankton (Michaelis-Menten with light co-limitation)                                                                                                                                             
 • Zooplankton grazing (Holling Type II)                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Mortality and nutrient recycling                                                                                                                                                                                         
 • Light limitation with self-shading (Beer-Lambert law)                                                                                                                                                                    

Key Observation:                                                                                                                                                                                                            

The model uses quadratic (density-dependent) mortality for zooplankton (m_Z * Z_prev * Z_prev) but linear mortality for phytoplankton (m_P * P_prev). This asymmetry may not capture realistic phytoplankton dynamics,      
especially at high densities where:                                                                                                                                                                                         

 1 Intraspecific competition for space and resources intensifies                                                                                                                                                            
 2 Viral lysis rates increase with host density                                                                                                                                                                             
 3 Aggregation and sinking losses accelerate                                                                                                                                                                                

Ecological Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                     

I propose adding density-dependent phytoplankton mortality to better represent these processes. This will:                                                                                                                  

 • Create more realistic bloom dynamics with self-limiting growth                                                                                                                                                           
 • Better capture the transition from exponential to stationary phase                                                                                                                                                       
 • Improve model stability at high phytoplankton concentrations                                                                                                                                                             
 • Maintain symmetry with zooplankton mortality structure                                                                                                                                                                   

The modification changes phytoplankton mortality from linear (m_P * P) to include a quadratic component (m_P * P + m_P2 * P²), where m_P2 represents the density-dependent mortality coefficient.

 +
