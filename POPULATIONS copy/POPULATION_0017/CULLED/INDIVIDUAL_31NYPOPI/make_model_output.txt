                                                                                                         Assessment                                                                                                         

Current Model Structure:                                                                                                                                                                                                    

The model simulates NPZ dynamics with:                                                                                                                                                                                      

 • Nutrient uptake by phytoplankton (Michaelis-Menten with light co-limitation)                                                                                                                                             
 • Zooplankton grazing (Holling Type II)                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Mixed linear-quadratic phytoplankton mortality                                                                                                                                                                           
 • Light limitation with self-shading                                                                                                                                                                                       
 • Nutrient recycling from mortality                                                                                                                                                                                        

Key Observation:                                                                                                                                                                                                            

The model includes light limitation but treats it as a simple co-limiting factor. In real oceanic systems, phytoplankton photoacclimation is crucial - phytoplankton adjust their cellular chlorophyll content in response  
to light availability, which affects both their growth efficiency and light attenuation properties. The current model uses fixed light parameters (k_p, K_I) that don't account for this dynamic adjustment.                

Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Add photoacclimation - a dynamic chlorophyll-to-carbon ratio that responds to light conditions. This is ecologically important because:                                                                                     

 1 Low light → higher chlorophyll per cell → better light harvesting but also more self-shading                                                                                                                             
 2 High light → lower chlorophyll per cell → reduced photodamage risk                                                                                                                                                       
 3 This creates a feedback: dense blooms increase self-shading, triggering photoacclimation, which further modifies light attenuation                                                                                       

This is a well-documented phenomenon in phytoplankton ecology and adds realism without excessive complexity.
