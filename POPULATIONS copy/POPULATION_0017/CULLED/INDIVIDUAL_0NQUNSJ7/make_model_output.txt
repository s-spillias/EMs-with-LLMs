Missing Ecological Process:                                                                                                                                                                                                 

A critical missing component in oceanic mixed layer models is light limitation. Phytoplankton growth depends not only on nutrients but also on light availability. In the current model, growth is only limited by nutrients
(Michaelis-Menten term), which is unrealistic for oceanic systems where light can be equally or more limiting, especially:                                                                                                  

 • At depth in the mixed layer                                                                                                                                                                                              
 • During different seasons                                                                                                                                                                                                 
 • With self-shading effects from high phytoplankton biomass

Implement light limitation with self-shading using a multiplicative limitation approach. This adds:                                                                                                                         

 1 A light attenuation parameter that accounts for how phytoplankton biomass reduces light penetration                                                                                                                      
 2 A combined nutrient-light limitation term that represents the minimum of the two resources (Liebig's Law of the Minimum) or their multiplicative interaction                                                             

This is ecologically justified because:                                                                                                                                                                                     

 • Light is a fundamental resource for photosynthesis                                                                                                                                                                       
 • High phytoplankton concentrations create self-shading                                                                                                                                                                    
 • The interaction between light and nutrients determines bloom dynamics                                                                                                                                                    
 • This mechanism is essential for realistic NPZ dynamics in oceanic mixed layers
