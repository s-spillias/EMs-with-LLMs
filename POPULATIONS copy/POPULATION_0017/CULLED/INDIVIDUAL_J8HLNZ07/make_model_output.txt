Current Model Structure:                                                                                                                                                                                                    

The model simulates nutrient-phytoplankton-zooplankton (NPZ) dynamics with:                                                                                                                                                 

 • Nutrient uptake by phytoplankton (Michaelis-Menten kinetics with light co-limitation)                                                                                                                                    
 • Zooplankton grazing (Holling Type II)                                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Mixed linear-quadratic phytoplankton mortality                                                                                                                                                                           
 • Light limitation with self-shading (Beer-Lambert law)                                                                                                                                                                    
 • Nutrient recycling from mortality                                                                                                                                                                                        

Key Observation:                                                                                                                                                                                                            

The model includes light limitation but treats it as a simple multiplicative factor. In reality, phytoplankton photoacclimation (physiological adjustment to light conditions) is a critical process in oceanic mixed       
layers. When phytoplankton experience variable light (due to mixing), they adjust their cellular chlorophyll content and photosynthetic efficiency. This creates a dynamic feedback: high light → lower chlorophyll per cell
→ reduced self-shading effect; low light → higher chlorophyll per cell → enhanced self-shading.                                                                                                                             

Proposed Improvement:                                                                                                                                                                                                       

Add a dynamic chlorophyll-to-carbon ratio (θ) that responds to light availability. This captures photoacclimation and creates a more realistic light-phytoplankton feedback. The chlorophyll ratio affects both light       
attenuation (self-shading) and photosynthetic efficiency.                                                                                                                                                                   

Ecological Justification:                                                                                                                                                                                                   

 1 Photoacclimation is well-documented in phytoplankton ecology                                                                                                                                                             
 2 Explains observed variability in chlorophyll:carbon ratios (typically 0.01-0.05 g Chl/g C)                                                                                                                               
 3 Creates realistic bloom dynamics where self-shading becomes more pronounced as cells acclimate to surface light                                                                                                          
 4 Improves model realism for mixed layer dynamics where cells experience light gradients                                                                                                                                   

This is a moderate complexity addition that significantly improves ecological realism without over-parameterization.
