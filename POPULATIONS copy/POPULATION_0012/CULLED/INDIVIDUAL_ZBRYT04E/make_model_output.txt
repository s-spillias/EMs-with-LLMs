Key Issues Identified:                                                                                                                          

 1 Missing Critical Outbreak Trigger: The model lacks a mechanism for coral-mediated larval survival. Literature strongly suggests that COTS

   dynamics and larval food availability.

   supply, Allee effects in corals themselves), but the current model doesn't capture this.

   working. The fecundity parameters seem reasonable but may need the outbreak to be triggered first.

reefs → more structural complexity → better retention of phytoplankton and nutrients → higher COTS larval survival → more COTS → coral decline →
reduced larval survival → COTS crash.                                                                                                           

This mechanism:                                                                                                                                 

 • Explains why outbreaks occur on healthy reefs                                                                                                
 • Creates realistic boom-bust dynamics                                                                                                         
 • Provides a natural crash mechanism when coral is depleted                                                                                    
 • Is well-supported by ecological literature on reef-plankton interactions
