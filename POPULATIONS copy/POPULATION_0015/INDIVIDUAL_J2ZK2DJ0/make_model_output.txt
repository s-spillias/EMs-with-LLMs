 +- Rationale: In mixed layers, phytoplankton biomass attenuates light via Beer-Lambert extinction, reducing growth as blooms develop. This negative feedback often improves model realism and stabilizes blooms without

 +  • Replace constant light limitation f_I with a time-varying, biomass-dependent term computed each time step:                                                                                                            
 +      I_eff(t) = I * exp(-k_shade * P(t-1))

 +  • Only the previous model state P(t-1) is used, avoiding data leakage.

 +  • Reduced growth at high P, limiting overprediction during blooms.
