   when zooplankton biomass is high, which often improves timing and amplitude of Z peaks without harming P bloom dynamics.

 2 Explanation of changes                                                                                                                                                                                                       

 • Why: In many NPZ datasets, models with Holling II/III alone can overpredict Z growth when Z is already abundant (predator crowding, interference, and prey refuge at high Z densities are missing). A Beddington–DeAngelis   
   interference term is a parsimonious, ecologically supported modification that mitigates this, often improving residuals for Z and stabilizing P-Z oscillations.                                                              
 • What changed mathematically: G(P,Z) becomes                                                                                                                                                                                  
   G = g_max * (P^h / (K_G^h + P^h)) * Z / (1 + gamma_interference * Z)                                                                                                                                                         
   where gamma_interference >= 0 with units (g C m^-3)^-1 so the denominator is dimensionless.                                                                                                                                  
 • Implementation details:                                                                                                                                                                                                      
    • Added PARAMETER(gamma_interference) with soft bounds [0, 10].                                                                                                                                                             
    • In the integration loop, compute interference = 1 + gamma_interference * pospart(Z_prev), and divide G by (interference + eps) to keep AD-safety and numerical stability when Z dips slightly negative (which is already  
      penalized).
