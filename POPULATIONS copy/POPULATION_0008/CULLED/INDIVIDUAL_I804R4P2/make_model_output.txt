 2 Explanation of changes                                                                                                                                                                                                       

 • Rationale: The current model regulates COTS via a logistic term tied to carrying capacity Kc(F,S) and a constant baseline mortality mC_base. This can produce smooth declines but often underestimates abrupt busts seen     
   after coral depletion when adult COTS face starvation. Introducing an intake-dependent mortality term mC_starv_max * (1 - I_sat)^p_starv increases mortality when food is scarce, producing faster outbreak collapses while  
   retaining the existing outbreak triggers (food, temperature, immigration, Allee).                                                                                                                                            
 • Mathematical change: In the COTS update, replace -mC_base*C with -(mC_base + mC_starv)*C, where mC_starv = mC_starv_max * (1 - I_sat_curr)^p_starv and I_sat_curr is the existing saturating food index derived from         
   model-predicted F,S at t-1. This respects the no data leakage constraint.                                                                                                                                                    
 • Parameters added:                                                                                                                                                                                                            
    • mC_starv_max (year^-1): maximum additional mortality rate under starvation.                                                                                                                                               
    • p_starv (dimensionless): exponent shaping how quickly mortality increases as intake declines.                                                                                                                             
 • Bound penalties were added to keep parameters in biologically plausible ranges.
