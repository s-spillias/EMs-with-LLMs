 1 Are code changes needed?                                                                                                                                                                                                     

Yes. To better capture episodic boom-bust dynamics and avoid overcoupling of prey limitation across unrelated processes, I propose one ecological improvement: replace the current Type-II grazing response with a flexible     
Type-III grazing functional response and decouple its half-saturation from the prey limitation used in COTS reproduction/settlement. This adds two parameters (K_graze, theta_graze) and modifies the grazing term in model.cpp.
No data leakage is introduced; only previous time-step states and forcings are used.                                                                                                                                            

 2 Explanation of needed changes                                                                                                                                                                                                

 • Rationale: The current model uses a single prey saturation term (K_prey) for both COTS demographic processes and coral grazing, with a Type-II form for grazing. This can limit the model’s ability to reproduce sharp       
   transitions (outbreak onset and crash) seen in the PROJECT CONTEXT. A Type-III functional response for grazing introduces a refuge at low coral cover (reducing per-capita grazing when prey is scarce), which strengthens   
   the bust phase and enables recovery of coral cover that can later fuel new outbreaks. Decoupling half-saturation for grazing (K_graze) from that for COTS demography (K_prey) allows the data to inform these processes      
   independently.                                                                                                                                                                                                               
 • Mathematical change: Replace percap_graz = g_max * prey_frac with percap_graz = g_max * (avail^theta_graze) / (K_graze^theta_graze + avail^theta_graze).                                                                     
 • Parameters added:                                                                                                                                                                                                            
    • K_graze: half-saturation of weighted coral prey for COTS grazing (% cover)                                                                                                                                                
    • theta_graze: shape exponent (>=1) controlling Type-III behavior                                                                                                                                                           
 • Implementation details:                                                                                                                                                                                                      
    • Preserve numerical stability with eps floors and caps.                                                                                                                                                                    
    • Do not use current time-step observations; only predicted states from t-1 are used.
